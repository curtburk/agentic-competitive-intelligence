## Overview

The Dell Pro Max with GB10 is a compact, desktop-form factor AI development appliance powered by the NVIDIA Grace Blackwell GB10 Superchip. It is designed for developers, researchers, and data scientists who need to prototype, fine-tune, and run inference on large AI models locally without relying on cloud infrastructure or shared remote resources. It serves as a 'mini AI supercomputer' that bridges the gap between desktop development and data center-scale AI.
