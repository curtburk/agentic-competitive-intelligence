## Details

Lenovo is aggressively expanding its AI-ready laptop lineup beyond its high-end P series, introducing NPU-enabled models like the ThinkPad X13, L14, and L16 featuring Intel Core Ultra and AMD Ryzen AI Pro processors. This 'world-first' marketing for the ThinkStation P4 and broad AI laptop push indicates a strategic shift to capture the mainstream professional market for on-device AI, directly competing with HP's EliteBook and ZBook lines.
