## Details

Lenovo released the ThinkPad P16 Gen 3, a high-performance mobile workstation designed for local AI workloads. It features an Intel Core Ultra 9 285HX CPU with a dedicated NPU and NVIDIA Blackwell GPUs, supporting up to 192 GB of RAM. The device is positioned as a rugged, workstation-class laptop for engineers and data scientists requiring significant VRAM and TOPS for generative AI tasks.
