## Details

Lenovo launched the ThinkPad P16s Gen 4, a mid-range mobile workstation featuring Intel Core Ultra processors and NVIDIA RTX Pro 500 series GPUs. The system supports up to 96 GB of memory and includes ISV certifications and MIL-STD 810H durability testing, targeting professional users who need reliable performance in a portable form factor.

## Specs Mentioned

- **CPU**: Intel Core Ultra 7 265H (vPro) or Core Ultra 9 285H
- **GPU**: NVIDIA RTX Pro 500 6GB
- **Memory**: Up to 96 GB
- **Base Price**: $1,599
- **As-Tested Price**: $4,218
