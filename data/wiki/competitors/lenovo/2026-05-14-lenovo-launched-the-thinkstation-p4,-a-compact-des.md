## Details

Lenovo launched the ThinkStation P4, a compact desktop workstation featuring AMD Ryzen Pro 9000 Series processors (up to 16-core Ryzen 9 Pro 9965X3D with 3D V-Cache), up to 256 GB of memory, and factory liquid cooling. This positions Lenovo to compete in the mainstream professional workstation segment with a focus on high-density memory and thermal efficiency in a small form factor.

## Specs Mentioned

- **CPU**: AMD Ryzen Pro 9000 Series (Zen 5), up to Ryzen 9 Pro 9965X3D (16-core)
- **Memory**: Up to 256 GB
- **Cooling**: Factory liquid cooling
- **Form Factor**: Compact desktop
