# NVIDIA - Competitor Profile

**Last Updated**: 2026-05-13
**Threat Level**: High
**Tiers**: Fury, Nano

---

## Overview

NVIDIA is both a supplier (GB10/Blackwell chips) and a competitor (DGX products). Their first-party products set the benchmark that OEMs like HP compete against on price, support, and ecosystem.

## Products

- **DGX Station** (Fury competitor) - NVIDIA's flagship AI workstation
- **DGX Spark** (Nano competitor) - NVIDIA's compact AI desktop

## Key Channels to Monitor

- nvidia.com/en-us/products/workstations
- GTC announcements
