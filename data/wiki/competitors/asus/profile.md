## Overview

The Asus Ascent GX10 is a compact desktop 'personal AI supercomputer' designed to make advanced AI development accessible to developers, AI researchers, and data scientists. It is engineered for local AI training, inference, and model fine-tuning, allowing users to run large AI models without relying on cloud infrastructure or large-scale data center equipment.
