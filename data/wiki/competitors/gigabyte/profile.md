## Overview

The Gigabyte AI TOP ATOM is a 'Personal AI Supercomputer' and compact desktop workstation designed for local AI development. It is positioned as a solution for running demanding AI workloads on-premises, eliminating the need for cloud dependency. It targets AI developers, enthusiasts, students, researchers, and data scientists who need to perform prototyping, fine-tuning, and inference locally.
