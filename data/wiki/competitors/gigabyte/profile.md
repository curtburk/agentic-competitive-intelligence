## Overview

The GIGABYTE W775-V10-L01 is a powerful deskside AI workstation and supercomputer built on NVIDIA’s Grace Blackwell architecture. It combines the CPU and GPU into a unified platform with massive coherent memory to enable local training and deployment of large AI models. It is designed for AI engineers, data scientists, and enterprise users seeking data-center-class compute in a compact, on-premise solution.
