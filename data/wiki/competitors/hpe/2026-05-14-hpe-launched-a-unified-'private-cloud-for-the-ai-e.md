## Details

HPE launched a unified 'Private Cloud for the AI Era' stack, consolidating Morpheus, Zerto, and Alletra MP into a single operating model. The offering includes three form factors: PC 1000 (hyperconverged), PC 3000 (disaggregated), and PC 7000 (fully managed as-a-service). This positions HPE to capture enterprise AI infrastructure workloads by simplifying deployment and offering an exit path from VMware.
