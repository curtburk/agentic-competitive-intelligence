## Overview

The Supermicro Super AI Station (Model: ARS-511GD-NB-LCC) is a deskside AI supercomputer designed to bridge the gap between consumer-grade PCIe GPU workstations and data-center grade AI servers. It brings the NVIDIA GB300 Grace Blackwell Ultra Superchip platform to the desktop. It is targeted at developers and enterprises requiring secure, on-premises compute for building autonomous agents, running trillion-parameter models locally, and performing AI model training/fine-tuning without data privacy risks.
